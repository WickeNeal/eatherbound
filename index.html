THE OATH

NO MICROTRANSACTIONS.
JUST SKILL.

We grew up in an era where legendary armor was earned, not bought. Eatherbound returns to those roots. No pay-to-win mechanics. No time-gated energy. Just you, your party, and the Abyss. If you see someone wearing the Abyssal Plate, know that they bled for it.

01 // THE WORLD

THE SANCTUARY &
THE ABYSS

The Hub City

Between runs, return to the Sanctuary. A sprawling pixel-art metropolis where players gather. Accept quests from the guild, trade with merchants, and form parties before venturing out.

Procedural Dungeons

Step through the Gateways to enter the Abyss. Each dungeon is procedurally generated based on your party size. Expect 20-30 minute intense runs ending in epic boss battles.

02 // COMBAT ARTS

SOULS-LIKE
TACTICS

Tactical & Visceral

Combat requires precision. Master the art of the Parry, manage your stamina for Dodges, and unleash active abilities unique to your class. It's not just about button mashing; it's about timing and strategy.
